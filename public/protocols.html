Security Protocol

  1. Log out after each use:
    • Ensure that you log out once you finish using the system to prevent anyone from having free access to sensitive information.
  2. Use strong passwords:
    • Your password must be at least 8 characters, at least 1 uppercase, at least 1 lowercase, at least 1 number, and at least 1 symbol. Ensure your password is easy to remember but secure enough to prevent a breach.
  3. Breach Response:
    • If a security breach is detected:
      • Do not use the system until the breach has been cleared. The system will automatically log out every user and will be locked until the breach has been cleared.
      • Contact the IT department immediately if you have an emergency and need a patientâ€™s information. The system will automatically send a breach warning to the IT department but, contact them if the information is needed is urgent.
      • Wait for instructions on re-authenticating into the system.
  4. Do not give out any information:
    • No one other than yourself should know your username and password. Any person who knows this sensitive information may have a higher chance of a system breach.
